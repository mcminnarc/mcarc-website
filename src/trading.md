# Trading Post

Buy, sell, and trade ham radio and related equipment with fellow club members!

## How to List an Item

Send your item information to [kg4fzr@yahoo.com](mailto:kg4fzr@yahoo.com) including:

- Item description
- Price (or trade interest)
- Your phone number
- Your email address

**We'll post your item for one month free!**

<div class="info-box">
<strong>Support the Club:</strong> If the Trading Post helped you sell your item(s), please consider a small donation to help offset the cost of maintaining our web presence.
</div>
